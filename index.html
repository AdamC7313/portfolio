Hi, I'm Adam.

I'm an aspiring web developer learning a suite of languages and frameworks that will allow me to create beautiful and responsive websites and single-page applications.

I've always loved a challenge.

Science and music have always been my passion. Both bring a sense of challenge and need for practice. I've always been pushed to be the best I can be.

So naturally, I went to college (Florida Southern) to get a degree in biology and pursue medical school. I quickly found out that this path was not mine.

After my bachelor's, I went to the University of South Florida for a master's in medical science. Realizing the exponential growth of the clinical research industry, I got a job as a coordinator at a local site.

Spending a year as a coordinator taught me about clinical research and prepared me for my next job as a vendor specialist for a large network of sites.

I found one thing to be my favorite part about both of these jobs: I created an excel app that helped me do my job better.

I decided to begin teaching myself how to code using any resources I could find, creating projects and websites on my own.

Now my goal is to help others by building them tools to help them do their jobs easier.

My background is mainly scientific, specifically in lab and clinical research work, however, I am a quick learner and easy to work with.

I will work to bring your ideas to life exactly how you envision them.
